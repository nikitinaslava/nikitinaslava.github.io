PPC manager

We are currently looking for a PPC Specialist who will be involved in developing promotion strategy for the project.

Apply to this position

What to do:

  • Whitelisting (GAds, Meta, etc);
  • Collect competitors advertising semantics;
  • Regular competitors analysys;
  • Launch, monitor, optimize and scale search, display, remarketing, and video campaigns using AdNetworks;
  • Set up conversion tracking alongside our web analyst;
  • Formulate and test hypotheses targeted at increasing the efficiency of campaigns;
  • Develop promotion strategies aimed at achieving set goals;
  • Identify improvement areas;
  • Cooperate with brand, content, website, product teams;
  • Prepare weekly, monthly and quarterly reports on the campaign’s efficiency;

You’re the one we’re looking for if:

  • You have at least two years of experience working with G Ads, FB and other AdNetworks (and can prove it with successful case);
  • You’re proficient in Google Analytics & GTM;
  • You know English at least on an Upper-Intermediate level;
  • You can operate with high volumes of data;
  • You know what ROMI, CTR, CPC, CPM, CPA, CPL, LTV, and other abbreviations mean;
  • You know how to use Spy services like a pro and do it regularly.

Will be an Extra:

  • Knowladge of UI/UX principles;
  • Adwords certification;
  • Experience working with Hotjar.
